UFOs and those who investigate them come with a certain stigma surrounding them. While much of the UFO environment has this connotation there is some hard facts within this field. Most of the facts hold true due to the fact that UFOs at their core are simply unidentified. The assumption by the general public is that UFOs inately means extra extra terrestrial. By accepting that the incident is simply unidentified opens the door for the encounter to be much more real. The release government files and videos drives this point home. As far as true investigators are concerned the object can be man made and yet still be unknown. Several Government agencies have begun to release information and remove the negative stigma surrounding UFOs.

In 2017 three videos were leaked via New York Times Online. The videos claimed to be footage from Navy aircraft. Four years later in 2021 The DoD finally confirmed their authenticity. About this time an ex-DoD employee, Luis Elizondo, came forth about the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program (AATIP). Luis Headed the program since its black book inception in 2012. He left the program after receiving limited support for acting on these investigations and removing the stigma from reporting them. Since then there has been a push in Congress to release more files on UFOs that the government's intellegence agencies have. A special report was released in early 2022 making these files public. The release of these videos has also cuased congress to press the armed forces for more information and create a reporting path for when military personel have these kind of encounters.
